About

Lingator is a translation service designed to help navigate legal and medical systems. Our goal is to help users have a clear understanding of their rights, healthcare, and options available to them.

Many immigrants face barriers as they are unfamiliar with the Canadian law and medical procedures due to language barriers and complex terminology. Lingator strives to bridge this gap by providing accurate and intuitive translations and practical next steps, empowering users to make informed decisions.

Our goal is to ensure that English as a second language is never a barrier to understanding essential legal and medical information.
